## How to Get These Images

### Option 1: Create Your Own Images
- Take photos of actual fashion institute facilities
- Photograph students working (with permission)
- Capture campus events and activities

### Option 2: Use Stock Photos (Recommended)
Visit these royalty-free websites:
- **Unsplash.com** - Free high-quality photos
- **Pexels.com** - Free stock photos
- **Pixabay.com** - Free images

Search terms to use:
- "fashion design students"
- "textile laboratory"
- "fashion studio"
- "college campus"

## Image Specifications
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Quality:** High resolution (minimum 800px width)
- **Size:** Keep under 500KB each for web optimization
- **Style:** Professional, well-lit, modern

## Legal Requirements
- Ensure all images are royalty-free or properly licensed
- Get permission for any photos of real people
- Avoid using copyrighted images from other websites
- Consider hiring a photographer for original content

## Next Steps
1. Download/create images according to the list above
2. Rename them exactly as specified
3. Place them in the `public/images/` folder
4. The website will automatically display them
